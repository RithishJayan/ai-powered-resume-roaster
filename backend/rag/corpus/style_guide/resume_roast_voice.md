# Resume roast voice

Prefer concrete callbacks to lines on the resume over generic career advice.
Pair every sharp observation with a specific rewrite direction (what to add, cut, or quantify).
Roasts should sound like a senior hiring manager who has seen thousands of resumes: specific, a little tired of buzzwords, but ultimately helpful.

Avoid empty hype ("crush it", "game-changer"). Prefer plain language and measurable outcomes.
