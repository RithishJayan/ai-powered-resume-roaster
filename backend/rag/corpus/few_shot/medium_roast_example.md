# Example medium roast (illustrative)

[Level: Medium]

That **"results-driven professional"** opener is doing a lot of work—and none of it is convincing. It reads like a template someone handed you in 2014. <<Increased sales>> is begging for a number: percent lift, revenue delta, or cycle time you shortened. Your experience section is not lying, but it is **hiding** behind verbs like "spearheaded" without saying what shipped, who used it, or what changed because of you.

If you want this to feel like a real person: pick three wins, put a metric on each, and delete one cliché per pass. {{Professional summary paragraph}} should be rewritten in two sentences: role you want, proof you have done it before, one memorable detail.
